Fire Suppression System Singapore – Installation & Servicing

A reliable fire suppression system Singapore is essential for protecting kitchens, server rooms, data centres, factories and high-risk environments. Suppression systems activate automatically to control or extinguish fires before they spread, minimising downtime and property damage.

Engg-Tech provides installation and maintenance of kitchen hood suppression systems, clean agent systems (FM-200, Novec), CO₂ suppression, water mist systems and industrial fire suppression setups. These systems detect heat, flame or smoke and release the appropriate extinguishing agent within seconds.

Types of Fire Suppression Systems

  • Kitchen hood fire suppression (wet chemical)
  • Clean agent suppression (FM-200 / Novec 1230)
  • CO₂ fire suppression
  • Water mist systems
  • Industrial machinery suppression

A properly designed fire suppression system in Singapore reduces fire impact, protects equipment and safeguards occupants in commercial and industrial spaces. Regular servicing is required for SCDF compliance and reliable activation.
