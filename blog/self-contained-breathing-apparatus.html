Self Contained Breathing Apparatus Singapore – SCBA for Fire Safety & Rescue

A self contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) is essential for entering smoke-filled, toxic or oxygen-deficient environments. It provides clean, breathable air to firefighters, rescue teams and workers operating in hazardous areas.

Engg-Tech supplies SCDF-compliant SCBA sets commonly used for fire response, confined space entry, industrial emergencies, tunnel works and chemical exposure zones. Each unit includes a high-pressure cylinder, face mask, harness and pressure gauge designed to deliver safe and reliable airflow.

Common Uses of SCBA

  • Firefighting operations
  • Rescue and evacuation work
  • Confined space entry (e.g., tanks, manholes)
  • Industrial gas, chemical or toxic environments

A reliable self contained breathing apparatus in Singapore ensures worker safety by preventing inhalation of smoke or toxic gases. Proper training, regular inspection and timely replacement of parts are crucial for maintaining SCBA performance.
